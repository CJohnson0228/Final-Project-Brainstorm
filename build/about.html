About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to Paw Paw's Bakery! We are excited to provide the community with a professional, dedicated, and caring bakery that is looking forward to providing awesome service for you and your fur baby. Here at Paw Paw's Bakery it is our mission to make sure we are creating treats that are delicious, full of love, and healthy in order to provide a product that you will not have any worries about giving to your pets. We want to make sure that you receive a product that is guaranteed fresh and is made with the finest natural ingredients available. There are no artifical or additives added to our treats that will be harmful to your best friend.

What We are all About

We at Paw Paw's Bakery are a family-owned business that has enjoyed being part of the local community here in Warner Robins for the last two years. We have always had few family cats and dogs that have enriched our lives so we have always been careful about what food and treats they receive. We decided to create a treat that we could control what was being used and making sure that our pets received the best ingredients. We often shared this product with family and friends who told us that our different treats are something that all pet owners should get a chance to experience so we decided to open our business.
